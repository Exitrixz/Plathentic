Amelanchier Canadensis

Canadian Serviceberry

Watering (L): Moderate, about 3-5 liters per week

Properties: Deciduous shrub or small tree, white flowers, edible berries

Edible: Berries are edible and used in jams and pies

Poisonous: No

Colors: White flowers, green leaves, red to purple berries

Preferred Season: Spring for flowers, summer for fruit

Found in Biome: Forest edges, woodland gardens

Possible Diseases: Rust, powdery mildew

Disease Treatment: Fungicides, prune affected branches

Pruning: Prune after flowering to shape and remove suckers

Fertilizer: Balanced fertilizer, applied in early spring
