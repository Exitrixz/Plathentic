Antirrhinum Majus

Common Snapdragon

Watering (L): Moderate, keep soil evenly moist

Properties: Annual or perennial herb, upright growth, showy flowers

Edible: No

Poisonous: No, but non-edible

Colors: Various colors including red, pink, yellow, white

Preferred Season: Spring to summer for flowering

Found in Biome: Gardens, meadows

Possible Diseases: Rust, fungal leaf spots

Disease Treatment: Fungicides, remove affected foliage
