Acorus Calamus

Sweet Flag

Watering (L): 2-3 liters per day

Properties: Perennial herb, long, narrow leaves, aromatic rhizomes

Edible: Yes, rhizomes used in culinary and medicinal purposes

Poisonous: No

Colors: Green leaves

Preferred Season: Spring to autumn

Found in Biome: Wetlands, marshes, streambanks

Possible Diseases: Leaf spot, root rot

Disease Treatment: Fungicides, improve air circulation and drainage

Pruning: Trim dead leaves and divide clumps in early spring

Fertilizer: General-purpose liquid fertilizer during growing season
