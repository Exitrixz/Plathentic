Aloe Vera

Burn Plant

Watering (L): 0.5-1 liters per week

Properties: Succulent, medicinal plant, fleshy leaves with healing gel

Edible: Yes, but only the gel inside the leaves

Poisonous: Yes, the latex (yellow sap) can be toxic if ingested

Colors: Green leaves, yellow or orange flowers

Preferred Season: Spring and summer

Found in Biome: Desert, arid regions, indoor pots

Possible Diseases: Root rot, fungal stem rot, aloe mite

Disease Treatment: Remove affected parts, ensure proper drainage, use insecticides for mites

Pruning: Remove dead or damaged leaves as needed

Fertilizer: Diluted balanced fertilizer once a month during growing season
