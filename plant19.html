Ageratum Houstonianum

Flossflower

Watering (L): Regular watering, keep soil moist but not waterlogged

Properties: Annual herb, compact growth, fluffy clusters of small flowers

Edible: No

Poisonous: Non-toxic

Colors: Blue, purple, pink, white flowers

Preferred Season: Spring to fall

Found in Biome: Gardens, borders, containers

Possible Diseases: Powdery mildew, damping off

Disease Treatment: Fungicides, improve air circulation

Pruning: Deadhead spent flowers to promote new blooms

Fertilizer: Balanced fertilizer every 4-6 weeks during growing season
