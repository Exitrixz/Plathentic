Agapanthus Praecox

Blue Lily

Watering (L): 3-4 liters per day

Properties: Evergreen perennial, strap-like leaves, clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers

Edible: No

Poisonous: No

Colors: Blue, purple, or white flowers, green leaves

Preferred Season: Summer

Found in Biome: Gardens, borders, coastal areas

Possible Diseases: Leaf spot, crown rot

Disease Treatment: Fungicides, proper watering and drainage

Pruning: Deadhead spent flowers and remove old leaves in late autumn

Fertilizer: Balanced, slow-release fertilizer in spring and summer
