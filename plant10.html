Fernleaf Yarrow

Watering (L): 1-2 liters per day

Properties: Perennial herb, fern-like foliage, large flat-topped clusters of flowers

Edible: No

Poisonous: No

Colors: Bright yellow flowers, green leaves

Preferred Season: Summer

Found in Biome: Meadows, grasslands, gardens

Possible Diseases: Powdery mildew, rust
