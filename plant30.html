Alstroemeria Ligtu

Peruvian Lily

Watering (L): Moderate, about 3-5 liters per week

Properties: Perennial herb, tuberous roots, colorful flowers

Edible: No

Poisonous: No, non-toxic to humans and pets

Colors: Various colors including yellow, orange, red, pink, purple, or white flowers

Preferred Season: Spring and summer

Found in Biome: Gardens, borders, sunny locations

Possible Diseases: Botrytis blight, powdery mildew

Disease Treatment: Fungicides, remove infected plant parts

Pruning: Deadhead spent flowers to promote blooming

Fertilizer: Balanced fertilizer, applied in early spring and summer
