Allium Sativum

Garlic

Watering (L): 1-2 liters per day

Properties: Perennial herb, bulbous plant, strong-smelling, edible

Edible: Yes

Poisonous: No

Colors: Green leaves, white or purple bulbs

Preferred Season: Planted in fall, harvested in summer

Found in Biome: Cultivated fields, gardens

Possible Diseases: White rot, rust, downy mildew

Disease Treatment: Crop rotation, fungicides
