Angelica Archangelica

Garden Angelica

Watering (L): High, keep soil consistently moist

Properties: Biennial herb, large aromatic leaves, hollow stems

Edible: Yes, young stems and roots used in culinary and medicinal purposes

Poisonous: No, but avoid consuming large quantities of seeds and leaves

Colors: Green foliage, small greenish-white flowers in clusters

Preferred Season: Spring to early summer for harvesting

Found in Biome: Meadows, damp woodland edges

Possible Diseases: Leaf spot, powdery mildew

Disease Treatment: Organic fungicides, prune affected parts

Pruning: Remove spent flower stems to encourage growth

Fertilizer: Balanced fertilizer during active growth
