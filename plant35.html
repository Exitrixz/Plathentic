Amorphophallus Titanum

Titan Arum

Watering (L): High humidity, water regularly to keep soil moist

Properties: Giant flowering plant, produces largest unbranched inflorescence in the world

Edible: No, produces strong odor to attract pollinators, smells like rotting flesh

Poisonous: No, but handle with care due to size and potential irritation

Colors: Reddish-purple on spadix, green on spathe

Preferred Season: Blooms irregularly, often every few years in summer

Found in Biome: Rainforests of Sumatra, cultivated in botanical gardens

Possible Diseases: Fungal infections due to high humidity

Disease Treatment: Fungicides, improve air circulation

Pruning: Remove dead leaves and flowers after bloom cycle

Fertilizer: Balanced fertilizer during active growth, reduce during dormancy
