Anethum Graveolens

Dill

Watering (L): Moderate, keep soil evenly moist

Properties: Annual herb, feathery leaves, aromatic

Edible: Yes, leaves and seeds used in cooking

Poisonous: No, but may cause mild skin irritation in some people

Colors: Yellow flowers, green foliage

Preferred Season: Spring to summer for leaves, late summer for seeds

Found in Biome: Gardens, cultivated fields

Possible Diseases: Powdery mildew, aphids

Disease Treatment: Neem oil, improve air circulation

Pruning: Trim flowers to prolong leaf production

Fertilizer: Balanced fertilizer, apply during active growth
