Ageratum Conyzoides

Billygoat-Weed

Watering (L): Regular watering, keep soil evenly moist

Properties: Perennial herb, upright growth, clusters of small, fluffy flowers

Edible: No

Poisonous: Non-toxic, but can cause skin irritation in some individuals

Colors: White, occasionally pink or purple flowers

Preferred Season: Year-round in tropical climates

Found in Biome: Tropical and subtropical regions, waste areas, roadsides

Possible Diseases: Leaf spot, fungal infections

Disease Treatment: Fungicides, improve air circulation

Pruning: Remove spent flowers and trim back leggy growth as needed

Fertilizer: Balanced fertilizer every 4-6 weeks during growing season
