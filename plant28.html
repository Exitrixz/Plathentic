Aloe Arborescens

Candelabra Aloe

Watering (L): Low, about 2-3 liters per week

Properties: Succulent shrub, upright stems, red-orange tubular flowers

Edible: No

Poisonous: No, but caution with the latex can cause skin irritation

Colors: Green leaves, red-orange flowers

Preferred Season: Spring and summer

Found in Biome: Arid regions, gardens, rocky slopes

Possible Diseases: Root rot, aphids

Disease Treatment: Improve drainage, use insecticidal soap for aphids

Pruning: Remove dead or damaged leaves

Fertilizer: Low-nitrogen fertilizer in spring and summer
