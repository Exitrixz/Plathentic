Wood Anemone

Watering (L): Low to moderate, keep soil evenly moist

Properties: Perennial herb, spreading by rhizomes

Edible: No

Poisonous: No, but can cause skin irritation

Colors: White flowers, sometimes pink or blue tinged

Preferred Season: Spring

Found in Biome: Deciduous woodlands, shady gardens

Possible Diseases: Powdery mildew, leaf spot
